Positioning

Codie Capital Research is a personal investment journal and portfolio record by Codie Marillier. It is not a fund, advisory service, investment service, or money-management business.

The site documents Codie's own portfolio, weekly reviews, decisions, mistakes, lessons, and investment philosophy. It is designed for accountability, family and friends, future employers, business contacts, potential mentors, and Codie himself.

  • Not investment advice.
  • Not FCA-authorised.
  • Does not manage money for other people.
  • Does not ask visitors to invest.
  • Does not recommend copying trades.
